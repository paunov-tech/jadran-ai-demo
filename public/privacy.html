Privacy Policy

How JADRAN.AI collects, uses, and protects your information

LAST UPDATED: APRIL 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SIAL Consulting d.o.o. ("we", "our", "us") operates the JADRAN.AI service, available at jadran.ai and as an Android application. By using JADRAN.AI, you agree to this policy.

1. Who We Are

SIAL Consulting d.o.o.
Contact: info@jadran.ai

2. Information We Collect

Location Data

With your permission, we access your device's GPS coordinates to provide local weather forecasts, UV index, sea conditions, and nearby destination suggestions. Location is used in real time and is not stored on our servers.

Device Identifier

We generate and store a random device ID in your browser's local storage to manage your access plan (free, week, season, VIP) without requiring account registration. This ID is not linked to your personal identity.

Email Address

If you make a payment or submit a booking inquiry, we collect your email address solely to send confirmation and access recovery emails. We do not use it for marketing without your consent.

Payment Data

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number or payment details. Stripe's privacy policy applies: stripe.com/privacy.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymized data about how you interact with the app (pages visited, features used) to improve the service. This data cannot identify you personally.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide weather, sea, and travel information relevant to your location
  • To activate and verify your access plan
  • To send booking confirmations and transactional emails
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To respond to your inquiries sent to our contact addresses

4. Third-Party Services

JADRAN.AI uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Firebase (Google) — data storage and real-time sync
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • HERE Maps — routing and map tiles
  • Anthropic / OpenAI — AI assistant responses (queries are processed but not stored long-term)
AI queries: When you ask the AI assistant a question, your message is sent to our AI provider to generate a response. We do not store your conversation history on our servers beyond the current session.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email address and device ID for as long as your access plan is active, or up to 2 years from your last interaction. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for location access at any time (via device settings)
  • Request data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@jadran.ai.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to store your device ID and preferences. No tracking cookies from advertising networks are used. You can clear local storage at any time through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

JADRAN.AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
